# BRAND KNOWLEDGE: HIS HAIR

## 1. IDENTITY & VISION
- **Brand Name:** HIS HAIR
- **Tagline:** Hair Health & Restoration Specialists.
- **Vision:** To normalize and elevate men’s hair restoration in South Africa, transforming it from a hidden secret into a premium grooming standard.
- **Mission:** Providing undetectable, high-performance hair systems via a discreet, end-to-end specialist service.

## 2. THE COMPETITIVE GAP (The "Anti-Clunky" Framework)
- **Problem:** US-based sites (e.g., Hollywood Lace) are information-dense, clinical, and difficult to navigate. They lack "The Hook" for modern professionals.
- **Solution:** Minimalist UI/UX inspired by Hims and Manual. Large whitespace, bold typography (Serif/Sans-Serif mix), and high-contrast color palettes.
- **South African Market:** We bridge the gap between "Cheap Retail" and "Expensive Medical Clinics" by offering a Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) lifestyle experience.

## 3. THE END-TO-END JOURNEY (THE FUNNEL)

### Stage 1: The Hero (Identity & Hook)
- **Focus:** Identity, Discretion, and Professionalism.
- **UI:** A mobile-first landing page that feels like an exclusive club invitation.
- **Message:** "Restoring the Professional Edge through Science and Community."

### Stage 2: The Proof (The "Restoration in Motion" Section)
- **Focus:** Skepticism reduction via high-definition video.
- **Content:** Three key video types:
    1. **Science:** Macro-shots of the HD-Lace mimicking the scalp (The Invisible Edge).
    2. **Lifestyle:** Men in high-stakes environments (Gym, Boardroom, Social) showing natural movement.
    3. **Results:** The transformation reveal.

### Stage 3: The Discovery (The Match Quiz)
- **Focus:** Frictionless onboarding.
- **Logic:** 5-7 questions (Activity level, Desired style, Hair density, Maintenance preference).
- **Goal:** Provide a "Personal Restoration Profile" instead of a generic product page.

### Stage 4: The Implementation (Partners & Subscription)
- **The Partner Network:** A directory of "Verified Partner Barbers" in Cape Town, Sandton, and Durban for "Cut-in" services.
- **The Maintenance Club:** Recurring Shopify subscription for adhesives, scalp care, and solvents.

## 4. DESIGN & TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- **Color Palette:** Oxford Blue, Slate Grey, and Champagne Cream.
- **Theme Benchmark:** Shopify Impact or Prestige themes.
- **Key Feature:** "Before/After" reveal sliders and "Reveal" CSS animations that mimic the "unveiling" of a secret.
- **Logistics:** Discreet, unbranded packaging and private billing via South African gateways (PayStack/Peach Payments).

## 5. REVENUE CHANNELS
1. **High-Ticket:** Premium Hair Units (One-time purchase).
2. **Recurring:** The Maintenance Box (Monthly subscription).
3. **Lead Gen:** Virtual Consultations.
