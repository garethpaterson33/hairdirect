# HIS HAIR — 5-Sprint Project Roadmap

### Principle: Ship fast. Ship sharp. Every sprint has a single testable outcome.

---

## Sprint Overview

| Sprint | Name | Duration | Primary Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | The Hero | 2 weeks | Homepage live, brand visible, quiz CTA functional |
| 2 | The Proof | 2 weeks | Invisible Edge video section live, product pages functional |
| 3 | The Funnel | 2 weeks | Match Quiz live end-to-end, consultation booking live |
| 4 | The Ecosystem | 2 weeks | Partner Barber directory live, Maintenance Club active |
| 5 | The Engine | 2 weeks | SEO baseline, email flows, analytics, launch-ready |

**Total: 10 weeks to full MVP.**

---

## Sprint 1: The Hero
### *"Make the first impression irreversible."*

**Goal:** A live, brand-true homepage that communicates the HIS HAIR proposition in under 8 seconds.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] Shopify store created (Impact or Prestige theme purchased and installed)
- [ ] Design tokens set: Oxford Blue / Slate Grey / Champagne Cream colour variables
- [ ] Typography loaded: Serif headline font (suggest: Playfair Display or Canela) + Sans-serif body (Suisse Int'l or Inter)
- [ ] Hero section built: Full-bleed image/video placeholder + H1 + Subhead + "Find My Match" CTA button
- [ ] Sticky navigation bar (6 items) coded and responsive
- [ ] Footer coded (links, legal, payment badges)
- [ ] Password page replaced with branded holding page
- [ ] Domain connected (hishair.co.za)
- [ ] Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel installed
- [ ] Mobile-first QA passed (iPhone 14 + Samsung Galaxy S23 minimum)

**Success Criteria:**
> A cold viewer lands on the homepage and within 8 seconds understands: (1) what HIS HAIR does, (2) that it's premium, and (3) what their next action is.

**Blockers to resolve in Sprint 1:**
- Shopify plan selected and billing active
- Domain purchased and DNS access confirmed
- Brand font licences purchased or Google Fonts alternatives finalised
- Hero image/video placeholder content sourced (even stock for now)

---

## Sprint 2: The Proof
### *"Show them before they have to ask."*

**Goal:** The Invisible Edge video section is live and product pages exist with enough detail to support a purchase decision.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] Invisible Edge section built on homepage (3-video grid, dark background)
- [ ] Video placeholders replaced with final production files (or high-quality stock proxies for soft launch)
- [ ] Shopify Products created (minimum 3 SKUs for MVP: Light Density / Medium Density / Full Density)
- [ ] Product page template built: HD gallery + spec table + How To Order accordion + Add to Cart
- [ ] /systems collection page built with filter UI
- [ ] PayStack or Peach Payments integrated and test transaction completed
- [ ] Discreet packaging copy written and approved
- [ ] Product photography brief created (even if shoot is Sprint 3)

**Success Criteria:**
> A user can browse, select, and attempt to purchase a hair system end-to-end. Payment gateway processes a R1 test transaction successfully.

---

## Sprint 3: The Funnel
### *"Turn browsers into buyers."*

**Goal:** The Match Quiz is live and fully functional. The virtual consultation flow is operational.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] Match Quiz built (5-question flow — recommend Typeform embedded or custom Shopify section)
- [ ] Quiz logic maps to product recommendations (3 outcome profiles minimum)
- [ ] "Your Restoration Profile" result page designed and coded
- [ ] /consult page built with Calendly embed (or Cal.com)
- [ ] Consultation confirmation email written and automated
- [ ] Quiz completion → email capture → welcome email flow built (Klaviyo or Omnisend)
- [ ] FAQ page built (minimum 6 objection-killing answers)
- [ ] About page built (brand story, 300 words)

**Success Criteria:**
> 40% of users who start the quiz complete it. Every quiz completion results in a product recommendation and an email capture.

---

## Sprint 4: The Ecosystem
### *"Build the moat."*

**Goal:** The Partner Barber network and Maintenance Club are live — creating the recurring revenue and trust infrastructure that separates HIS HAIR from every competitor.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] /partners page built with city filter (Sandton / Cape Town / Durban)
- [ ] Minimum 2 Partner Barbers signed per city (6 total) — contracts in place
- [ ] Partner barber profile cards designed and populated
- [ ] /partners/apply form built and routed to internal intake
- [ ] Maintenance Club product built on Shopify (Recharge or Shopify Subscriptions)
- [ ] Subscription product page written and designed
- [ ] "Maintenance Box" contents finalised with supplier (adhesives, solvent, scalp care)
- [ ] Recurring billing tested end-to-end
- [ ] Customer Account page reviewed: order history + subscription management working

**Success Criteria:**
> A customer can subscribe to the Maintenance Club and manage their subscription without contacting support. At least 4 Partner Barbers are live on the directory.

---

## Sprint 5: The Engine
### *"Build the machine that runs while you sleep."*

**Goal:** SEO foundation, email automation, and analytics are operational. The store is launch-ready for a controlled first audience.

**Deliverables:**

- [ ] SEO: Meta titles + descriptions on all P0/P1 pages
- [ ] SEO: Schema markup on product pages (Product + Review schema)
- [ ] SEO: Blog structure created with 3 cornerstone articles briefed (not yet written)
- [ ] Email: Welcome series (3 emails) written and live in Klaviyo
- [ ] Email: Abandoned cart sequence (2 emails) live
- [ ] Email: Post-purchase onboarding sequence (3 emails — what to expect, barber booking, maintenance reminder)
- [ ] Analytics: GA4 conversion events configured (quiz start, quiz complete, add-to-cart, purchase, consult booked)
- [ ] Analytics: Heatmap tool installed (Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity — free tier)
- [ ] Performance: Lighthouse score ≥ 85 on mobile
- [ ] Legal: Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Refund Policy pages live (use SA-specific templates)
- [ ] Soft launch: 50-person private beta (personal network + 2 Partner Barbers each)
- [ ] Beta feedback collated and P0 issues resolved

**Success Criteria:**
> Store achieves a Lighthouse mobile score of 85+. First 3 organic orders placed within 2 weeks of soft launch. Email welcome series achieving >40% open rate.

---

## Post-Sprint 5: Phase 2 Priorities (not MVP, but plan for them)

- Content Hub / Blog (SEO compound interest)
- Before/After gallery page (anonymised, curated)
- Referral programme ("Refer a colleague, get a month free on your Maintenance Club")
- Social proof aggregation (anonymised Trustpilot / Google Reviews)
- WhatsApp Business integration for consultation triage
- Paid media: Meta + Google campaigns (once conversion rate on organic is ≥ 2%)
